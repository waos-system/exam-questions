## Key Research Findings (2019-2024)

### Format Evolution
1. **Duration Reduction**: 3 hours → 2 hours (33% shorter)
2. **Question Reduction**:
   - Reading: 42 → 30 questions
   - Listening: maintained
   - Speaking: 4 → 3 tasks
   - Writing: maintained

3. **Content Shifts**:
   - Less focus on rare vocabulary
   - More emphasis on inference and synthesis
   - Greater authenticity in speaking/listening materials
   - Clearer assessment of actual academic English use

### Cognitive Demand Changes
| Cognitive Level | 2019 | 2024 | Trend |
|-----------------|------|------|-------|
| Recall | 30% | 25% | Decrease |
| Comprehension | 30% | 30% | Stable |
| Application | 20% | 25% | Increase |
| Analysis/Synthesis | 15% | 20% | Increase |

### Test-Taker Implications
1. **Reading**: Faster processing required; gist before details
2. **Listening**: Rapid synthesis of multiple ideas
3. **Speaking**: Clear organization crucial; natural hesitations acceptable
4. **Writing**: Idea development more important than vocabulary complexity
