## Set 3: TOEFL Speaking Performance (50 questions)
**File**: `data/lang/en_toefl_speaking_001-050.json`
**Genre**: Integrated Speaking - TOEFL iBT
**Exam**: TOEFL
**Section**: Speaking
**Time**: 13 minutes
**Format**: 3 tasks (1 independent + 2 integrated)

### Task Distribution:

#### Task 1: Independent Speaking (15 questions)
- **Time**: 45 seconds response time
- **Type**: Personal preference/choice questions
- **Topics**: Education, lifestyle, values, priorities
- **Evaluation**: Fluency, vocabulary range, pronunciation
- **Question Range**: `en_toefl_speaking_001` to `en_toefl_speaking_015`

#### Task 2: Integrated Speaking (18 questions)
- **Time**: 30 seconds preparation + 60 seconds response
- **Type**: Reading + Listening + Speaking
- **Source**: Academic announcement + student conversation
- **Topics**: Campus policies, academic concepts
- **Evaluation**: Accuracy in summarizing, clarity of explanation
- **Question Range**: `en_toefl_speaking_016` to `en_toefl_speaking_033`

#### Task 3: Integrated Speaking (17 questions)
- **Time**: 20 seconds preparation + 60 seconds response
- **Type**: Listening + Speaking
- **Source**: Lecture segment + student conversation
- **Topics**: Academic concepts with student perspectives
- **Evaluation**: Understanding lecture main idea, explaining student view
- **Question Range**: `en_toefl_speaking_034` to `en_toefl_speaking_050`

### Assessment Criteria:
1. **Fluency & Coherence** (30%): Natural pacing, clear organization
2. **Lexical Range & Accuracy** (25%): Vocabulary variety and appropriateness
3. **Grammatical Range & Accuracy** (25%): Sentence structure correctness
4. **Pronunciation & Intonation** (20%): Clear production of sounds

### Key Features:
- Authentic speaking scenarios
- Mix of personal and academic content
- Time pressure simulations
- Integration of multiple language skills
- Evaluation framework based on TOEFL rubrics

## Set 4: TOEFL Writing Performance (50 questions)
**File**: `data/lang/en_toefl_writing_001-050.json`
**Genre**: Integrated Writing - TOEFL iBT
**Exam**: TOEFL
**Section**: Writing
**Time**: 50 minutes
**Format**: 2 tasks (1 integrated + 1 independent)

### Task Distribution:

#### Task 1: Integrated Writing (25 questions)
- **Time**: 20 minutes
- **Type**: Reading → Listening → Writing synthesis
- **Response Length**: 150-225 words
- **Source**: Academic passage (225-300 words) + Lecture (2-2.5 min)
- **Format**: Explain relationship between reading and lecture
- **Topic Categories**:
  - Environmental science (25%)
  - Biology & evolution (20%)
  - History & culture (20%)
  - Business & economics (15%)
  - Technology (20%)
- **Question Range**: `en_toefl_writing_001` to `en_toefl_writing_025`

#### Task 2: Independent Writing (25 questions)
- **Time**: 30 minutes
- **Type**: Opinion or preference essay
- **Response Length**: 300+ words
- **Prompt Types**:
  - Opinion/agree-disagree (35%)
  - Personal choice (30%)
  - Comparison (20%)
  - Problem/improvement (10%)
  - Definition/explanation (5%)
- **Question Range**: `en_toefl_writing_026` to `en_toefl_writing_050`

### Evaluation Criteria (Unified 0-30 scale):
1. **Task Fulfillment** (30%): Complete response to prompt
2. **Organization** (25%): Clear structure and progression
3. **Coherence/Cohesion** (20%): Connection between ideas
4. **Lexical Range** (15%): Vocabulary appropriateness and variety
5. **Grammatical Accuracy** (10%): Correct sentence structures

### Key Features:
- Authentic academic writing scenarios
- Integration of skill synthesis
- Time-constrained writing practice
- Real-world writing tasks
- Comprehensive evaluation frameworks
